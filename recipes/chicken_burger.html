Chicken Burger

Description

A chicken sandwich is a sandwich that typically consists of boneless, skinless chicken breast served between slices of bread, on a bun, or on a roll.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound extra-lean ground chicken
  • ½ cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs, divided
  • ½ small onion, finely grated
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Method

  1. Preparing Raw Chicken Patty

    Mix ground chicken, 1/4 cup bread crumbs, onion, egg, garlic, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Moisten hands and shape chicken mixture, 2 tablespoons at a time, into flat, oval-shaped patties. Spread remaining 1/4 cup bread crumbs into a shallow dish. Roll patties in bread crumbs to coat.

  2. Heat the oil

    Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  3. Fry the patty

    Cook patties in hot oil until deep brown in color on the bottom, 5 to 6 minutes. Turn patties and cook until other side is browned, 3 to 4 minutes more.
